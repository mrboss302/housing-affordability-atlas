Where can I afford to live on $150,000 a year?

On a household income of $150,000, a standard 10%-down scenario supports an estimated home price up to about $463,627 — keeping housing near 28% of gross income.

The assumptions behind this estimate

  • 10% down payment
  • 6.70% interest rate, 30-year fixed
  • Property tax 1.1%/yr, insurance 0.45%/yr, PMI 0.6%/yr of the loan
  • Housing capped near 28% of gross income

At the top of that budget (~$463,627), the estimated payment is about $3,500/month ($2,693 principal & interest + $425 tax + $174 insurance + $209 PMI).
