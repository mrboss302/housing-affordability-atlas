Frequently asked questions

How much income do you need to buy in Baltimore, MD?

For a typical home around $191,986 (Zillow ZHVI, 2026-05), the estimated payment is roughly $1,449/month and the income needed is near $62,100. A starter home (~$102,902) lowers that to about $33,300. Model your own scenario on Home Payment Atlas.

Is it cheaper to rent or buy in Baltimore?

Median rent runs about $1,786/month (36% of income), versus an estimated $1,449/month to own a typical home. Rent-vs-buy depends on how long you stay, your down payment, and maintenance. Compare scenarios on the Home Payment Atlas calculator.

Where do these Baltimore figures come from?

Home value and rent are from Zillow Research (ZHVI/ZORI, 2026-05). Income and population are from the U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year 2023). They are estimates for comparison, not appraisals. Zillow does not endorse this site.
