How this ranking is built

Rent burden is the Zillow ZORI rent index (state = median of city ZORI) divided by income, annualized. Every figure is an educational estimate. See the full methodology, then customize the math on the Home Payment Atlas mortgage calculator.

Frequently asked questions

Where does this ranking's data come from?

Home values and rent are from Zillow Research (ZHVI/ZORI), 2026-05; income is from the U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year 2023). Figures are estimates, not appraisals.

How is the ranking calculated?

Rent burden is the Zillow ZORI rent index (state = median of city ZORI) divided by income, annualized. Full details are on the methodology page.
