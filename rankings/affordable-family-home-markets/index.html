Frequently asked questions

Where does this ranking's data come from?

Home values and rent are from Zillow Research (ZHVI/ZORI), 2026-05; income is from the U.S. Census Bureau (ACS 5-year 2023). Figures are estimates, not appraisals.

How is the ranking calculated?

Uses Zillow 3-bedroom ZHVI (falling back to 4-bedroom or mid-tier where unavailable). Full details are on the methodology page.

About these estimates & data sources

Home values and rents reflect Zillow Research data (ZHVI and ZORI, 2026-05). Household income reflects U.S. Census ACS data. All figures are estimates for general comparison, not appraisals or quotes — confirm details before relying on them.
