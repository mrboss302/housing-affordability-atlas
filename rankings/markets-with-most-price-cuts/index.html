On the data roadmap

The Price-cut share is a directional index, not a guarantee. To keep this site trustworthy, we do not publish placeholder market-signal numbers. This ranking goes live once these Zillow Research datasets are imported:

  • For-sale inventory — more choice favors buyers; pending
  • Share of listings with a price cut — rising cuts favor buyers; pending
  • Days to pending — slower sales favor buyers; pending
  • Zillow Market Heat Index — lower heat favors buyers; pending

Frequently asked questions

Why isn't the Price-cut share live yet?

It depends on Zillow market-signal datasets (inventory, price cuts, days to pending, market heat) that are not yet imported. We publish numbers only when the underlying data is in place — no fabricated figures.

Is this index financial advice?

No. It is a directional, educational signal of market conditions, not a guarantee, prediction, or financial, mortgage, or real-estate advice.

What can I use right now?

The affordability rankings are fully live on Zillow home-value data — see the most affordable states and starter-home markets below, or open the mortgage calculator.
